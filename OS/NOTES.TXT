
CALLING CONVENTION FOR OS:
	PARAMETERS ARE PASSED ON THE STACK.
	THE CALLER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR STORING REGISTERS DUE TO THE SPEED OF 
	PUSH/POP COMPARED TO INX AND DCX OF SP, OR OTHER MEANS OF 
	MANIPULATING SP. 8-BIT RETURN VALUES ARE RETURNED IN A, WHILE 16-BIT
	RETURN VALUES ARE RETURNED IN HL.
